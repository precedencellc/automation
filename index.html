Sommerville Bookkeeping Automation

A private bookkeeping tool operated by Carl Sommerville.

What it does

This is a private, single-user application that reads the operator’s own receipt and sales-notification emails and records the corresponding expense and income transactions in the operator’s own QuickBooks Online companies. It is not offered to the public.

Connecting to QuickBooks

The application connects to QuickBooks Online using Intuit’s standard OAuth authorization. The operator initiates the connection from the application running on their own computer and approves access through Intuit’s consent screen. To connect or reconnect, the operator runs the authorization step within the application.

Disconnecting

Access can be revoked at any time from the operator’s Intuit account settings, or via the disconnect page.
