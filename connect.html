Connect to QuickBooks

Sommerville Bookkeeping Automation is a private, single-user application that connects to the operator’s own QuickBooks Online company using Intuit’s standard OAuth authorization.

How to connect or reconnect

  1. Run the application on your own computer and start the authorization step.
  2. The application opens Intuit’s official consent screen in your browser.
  3. Sign in to Intuit and approve access to your QuickBooks Online company.
  4. Intuit returns you to the application, which securely stores the connection so it can record your expenses and income.

You can repeat these steps at any time to reconnect or to connect a different company. Access can be revoked whenever you like — see the disconnect page.
