Privacy Policy

Application: Sommerville Bookkeeping Automation  |  Effective date: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Sommerville Bookkeeping Automation application (the “App”) handles information. The App is a private, single-user bookkeeping tool operated by Carl Sommerville (the “Operator”) for the Operator’s own businesses. It is not offered to, or used by, the general public.

1. Who this applies to

The App is used solely by its Operator to automate bookkeeping for businesses the Operator owns and controls. There are no other users, customers, or subscribers, and the App does not collect information from any third party.

2. What the App accesses

To do its job, the App reads:

  • Email messages in the Operator’s own Gmail and Microsoft Outlook mailboxes that represent purchase receipts, expense confirmations, and sales/payout notifications from vendors and selling platforms.
  • The Operator’s own QuickBooks Online company data, in order to create and reconcile expense and income transactions.

The App accesses these accounts only with the Operator’s explicit authorization, granted through Google, Microsoft, and Intuit’s standard OAuth consent screens, and only to the extent needed to record the Operator’s own financial transactions.

3. What the App does with the data

The App extracts transaction details (such as vendor, date, amount, and order number) from the Operator’s receipt and sales emails and records them as expenses and income in the Operator’s own QuickBooks Online companies, attaching the source document to each entry.

4. Where data is stored

The App runs locally on the Operator’s own computer. Working data (including a local ledger used to prevent duplicate entries and copies of source documents) is stored locally on that computer. The App does not operate a server, database, or cloud service that stores data on behalf of anyone else.

5. Data sharing

The App does not sell, rent, trade, or share any data with third parties. Data moves only between the Operator’s own connected accounts (Google, Microsoft, and Intuit) and the Operator’s own computer, for the sole purpose of recording the Operator’s bookkeeping. No advertising, analytics, profiling, or data-broker activity takes place.

6. Data retention and deletion

Data is retained locally only as long as needed for bookkeeping and tax recordkeeping. The Operator may delete the App’s local data at any time, and may revoke the App’s access to any connected account at any time through that provider’s security settings (for example, Google, Microsoft, or Intuit account connections).

7. Security

Access credentials (API keys and OAuth tokens) are stored in local configuration files that are excluded from version control and are not transmitted to any third party. Because the App runs on the Operator’s own machine, the security of the data depends on the security of that machine, which the Operator maintains.

8. Children’s privacy

The App is a business tool and is not directed to, or used by, children.

9. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date above reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to Carl Sommerville at carl.sommerville@gmail.com.
